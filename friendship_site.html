A Letter for You
✉️
Raat ke 1:40 baje...
Hey, Dekh, kuch baatein jo main aaj tak shayad tujhe bol nahi paaya, par honestly, you are one of the best people I have ever met. Raat ke 1:40 ho rahe hain aur yeh likhte waqt bas yahi socha ki kuch achha sa prepare karun. Itne ups and downs dekhe hain humne saath mein, to let it not be down again. I promise, main hamesha tere saath rahunga, as always. Bas ek chhoti si shikayat thi. Tu jaanti hai na, main bahut si cheezein properly convey nahi kar pata. Par genuinely kabhi kabhi thoda off feel hota hai. Pata nahi kyu, lekin kabhi kabhi lagta hai ki baat karna pehle jaisa nahi raha, jaise sab forcefully ho raha ho. Agar kabhi kuch ho, to tu mujhe bol sakti hai. Main nahi chahta ki tere mann mein kuch bhi daba hua rahe. Open rehna, utna hi achha hai. Aur baaki... thank you for everything. Yeh chhoti moti nok-jhok ko seriously mat liya kar 😭. Kabhi kabhi tension mein dimaag ka dahi ho jaata hai aur phir sabse zyada baat bhi tujhse hi hoti hai. Isi chakkar mein kabhi kabhi gussa bhi aa jaata hai. Baaki sach kahun to zyada kuch keh nahi paunga. Bas itna ki hamesha saath rehna. Chahe ladte rahein, behas karte rahein, par rehna. Yeh duriyaan ab aur nahi jheli jaati. Bolna to bahut kuch hai, par shayad thoda agle kisi din ke liye bacha leta hoon. 🙂
– Anirudh
